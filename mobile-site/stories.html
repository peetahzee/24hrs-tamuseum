Impacting Lives
Through aviation enrichment and mentoring, our youth participants consistently show improved self-confidence, self-discipline, and much more.

Too often, a student’s only exposure to STEM (science, technology, engineering, math) is through textbooks and videos shown in class. Not very fun. We believe learning math and science should be hands-on and experiential.
Capturing Their Attention
Flying has the ability to capture the attention of young minds like no other activity.
